The Benefits of Volunteering

Utah County is the proud sponsor of the Foster Grandparent and Senior Companion programs.  Volunteers serve Utah County children and home-bound seniors across Utah & Wasatch County.

As a volunteer you will receive:

Tax Free Stipend

Meal or Meal Reimbursement

Transportation or Mileage reimbursement

Ongoing Training

People who volunteer live longer with better health!

Requirements:

55 Years or Older

Serve a Minimum of 15 Hours Per Week

Income Eligible
